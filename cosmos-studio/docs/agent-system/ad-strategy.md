# COSMOS Studio — 6-Month Ad Strategy (6 Markets)

Objective: sell the studio's outputs (scroll sites first, then UGC packs) and the studio itself (reseller tier) in six markets, using our own UGC pipeline as proof — the ads ARE the product demo. Every hook below follows problem-agitation-solution; every character obeys `_config/markets/{id}.md` defaults and emerald-tablets law (Alex™ persona rules).

Shared affiliate angle (all markets): "Run our system on RunPod — click our link and we both save." Compute referral (`runpod.io/?ref=kupuri`, `vast.ai/?ref=kupuri`) rides in every landing CTA and video description; projected side revenue in `pricing.md`.

Shared KPI definitions: CPL = ad spend / qualified briefs received · CVR = paid down payments / qualified briefs · ROAS = collected revenue / ad spend (cash-collected basis, not booked).

---

## 1. US-English (us-en)

**Platform mix:** Meta 30% · Instagram 25% · TikTok 25% · YouTube 20%
**UGC character:** young diverse professional, natural home-office/café setting, en-US voice (per market config) — reads as a founder showing their own site, not an ad.
**Hooks (A/B/C):**
1. "Your agency quoted $30K and 4 months. Watch what we shipped in 6 days." (authority)
2. "I scrolled my competitor's website and it felt like a movie. Mine felt like a PDF." (problem-agitation)
3. "This landing page is the ad. You're inside the product right now." (meta/demo)
**Targeting:** business owners + marketing directors, interests: web design/Awwwards/branding; geo-tilt to Manhattan, Silicon Valley, Miami Beach, Austin, Scottsdale (per market config wealthy_targets).
**Calendar:**
| Month | Focus | Volume |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Scroll-site demo ads (3 UGC variants × 4 formats) + landing launch | 12 pieces |
| 2 | Retarget engagers with pricing/payment-plan ads; 2 case-study cuts | 8 |
| 3 | UGC-ads product line launch; lookalike audiences from briefs | 10 |
| 4 | Anime/documentary reels for reach; YouTube pre-roll on design channels | 8 |
| 5 | Reseller-tier push to agencies (LinkedIn-style copy on Meta) | 6 |
| 6 | Best-performer scale month: 2× budget on top 3 ads only | 4 |
**KPIs:** CPL ≤ $45 · CVR ≥ 8% · ROAS ≥ 4 by month 3.

## Budget governance
- Global cap set by Bambu per quarter; every market's spend flows through the same >$500 approval gate as production payments.
- Weekly: self-improve cron ingests ad KPI lessons (`cost_spike` / `client_revision` types) and proposes mix changes — never auto-applies.
- Kill rule: any ad below 0.7× market CPL target after $150 spend is paused automatically (n8n workflow).
